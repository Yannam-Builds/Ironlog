The public repository is not currently available for general commercial use.

Commercial use requires a separate agreement with the copyright holder.

## Commercial use that requires permission

This includes, without limitation:

- selling the app or any derivative
- selling the source code
- app-store distribution for revenue or business use
- white-label deployments
- using the code in a coaching business, gym platform, or SaaS
- internal business deployment
- paid consulting engagements built on the codebase
- bundling the app into another commercial product

## Available deal types

Commercial arrangements may include:

- non-exclusive commercial license
- exclusive commercial license
- white-label license
- source-code purchase
- full IP and asset acquisition

## What a full acquisition may include

A full acquisition can be negotiated to include some or all of:

- source code and project files
- release assets
- screenshots and store assets
- documentation
- branding assets
- domain names or social handles, if owned and included
- assignment of relevant IP rights
