# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We are committed to making participation in this project a respectful, harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior
- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent.
- Focus feedback on code and outcomes, not people.
- Help keep discussions welcoming and practical.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, insults, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Repeated disruptive behavior in issues, PRs, or discussions.
- Publishing private information without consent.

## Enforcement
Project maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or restrict access for violations.

## Reporting
Report concerns privately to:
- `conduct@ironlog.app`
