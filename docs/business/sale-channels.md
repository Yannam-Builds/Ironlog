# IRONLOG Sale Channels

## Best places to sell the full app, codebase, and project files

These are the highest-value channels if the goal is an outright sale of the IP or product asset, not just selling a code template.

| Channel | Best for | Typical fit for IRONLOG | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Acquire.com | startup and micro-SaaS acquisitions | High | Good if you package IRONLOG as a product business or near-launch asset |
| Flippa | broad marketplace for apps, sites, and digital assets | High | Large buyer pool, but quality varies and buyer education matters |
| Empire Flippers | brokered sales for stronger online businesses | Medium | Better after launch and revenue than at pure pre-revenue stage |
| FE International | higher-end brokered M&A | Low to Medium | Strong only if traction/revenue becomes meaningful |
| TransferSlot | startup acquisition marketplace | Medium | Better for product/startup framing than code-template framing |
| Website Closers | brokered deal channel | Medium | More useful once there is revenue or strategic story |
| BuySellMyApp | mobile app/source-code marketplace | Medium | Useful for code/product buyers, but often lower-ticket than full strategic acquisition |
| Direct strategic outreach | gyms, coaching brands, app portfolios | Very High | Often best path for maximizing value if buyer sees strategic fit |
| LinkedIn outbound to founders/operators | direct buyer discovery | High | Best for finding serious strategic acquirers |
| Indie founder communities | founder-to-founder asset sales | Medium | Useful for conversations and leads, less structured than marketplaces |

## Which channels matter most right now

For IRONLOG today, the best-first channels are:

1. direct strategic outreach
2. Acquire.com
3. Flippa
4. TransferSlot
5. BuySellMyApp

The more broker-heavy channels become more relevant after public launch, traction, and revenue.

## Suggested positioning per channel

### Strategic outreach

Pitch as:

- premium Android workout-tracking product
- near-launch acquisition
- white-label / coaching / gym-tech base

### Acquire.com / TransferSlot

Pitch as:

- product business in fitness software
- differentiated local-first training app
- premium Android-first launch-ready asset

### Flippa / BuySellMyApp

Pitch as:

- full mobile app acquisition with source, branding, release pipeline, screenshots, and docs
- not just code files
- stronger-than-template product with analytics, recovery, and backup flows

## What to prepare before listing

- acquisition memo
- valuation range
- product video
- screenshots
- feature list
- tech stack summary
- known limitations
- launch status
- monetization roadmap
- legal ownership and license position

## Red flags buyers will notice

- unclear ownership of brand or code
- non-standard licensing without explanation
- no retention or revenue data
- no privacy policy or terms if public launch is planned
- weak transfer package

## Best order of operations

If the goal is to maximize value:

1. make legal position clear
2. launch or soft-launch on Play
3. collect traction
4. list or outreach with metrics

If the goal is speed:

1. prepare memo and demo now
2. start strategic outreach now
3. list in parallel on Acquire.com and Flippa
